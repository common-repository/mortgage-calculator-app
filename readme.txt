== Description ==


An easy way to place a mortgage calculator on your Wordpress site.

###What you get when you use the Mortgage Calculator mortgage calculator

*  Place a mortgage calculator with a simple shortcode [mortgage-calculator]
*  Put mortgage calculator on any post or page.
*  mortgage calculator background color
*  mortgage calculator message


###Add mortgage calculator to any post or page

Whether you are writing a post about your company or another company, you can include a well formatted mortgage calculator.

###Quick Start Guide

* [mortgage calculator quick start guide at thinklandingpages.com] (http://www.thinklandingpages.com/mortgage-calculator/)

###Mortgage Calculator 

Add as mortgage calculator to your site as you like.  The Mortgage Calculator lets you create mortgage calculator and save them for when ever you are ready to use them.



== Installation ==


1. Upload `mortgage-calculator` to the `/wp-content/mortgage-calculator/` directory
1. Activate the mortgage calculator through the 'Mortgage Calculator' menu in WordPress
1. Click **Mortgage Calculator** on the admin menu to enable and set your options.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to know how to program or design? =

No.  The mortgage calculator does the programming and design.

= Is there a limit to the number of mortgage calculator I can create? =
No, unlimited mortgage calculator can be created.

= Can the mortgage calculator be put on my homepage? =
Yes, you can put mortgage calculator on your homepage by using the Mortgage Calculator mortgage calculator shortcode on you homepage.

= Can I put mortgage calculator on any post or page? =
Yes, use the mortgage calculator shortcode, [mortgage-calculator] on your pages and posts.
